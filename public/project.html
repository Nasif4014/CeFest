POSTER AND PROJECT SHOWCASING

Eligibility:

Participant must be an undergraduate student and enrolled in any university studying under any discipline. Each team can submit only one poster or project or both.

Registration Fee:

  • For individual Participation, Registration fee is Four Hundred Taka (400)
  • For a team with 2 members, Registration fee is Eight Hundred Taka (800)
  • For a team with 3 members, Registration fee is Twelve Hundred Taka (1200)

Rules and regulations:

  • Maximum participants for each team cannot be more than three (03).
  • Each team can submit only one poster or project (Model /Prototype) or both.
  • Topic of poster or project should be related to civil engineering.
  • The poster or project must contain a title.
  • Each team is required to have their specific team name
  • The full name of the person or persons of a team should be included in the poster.
  • Poster size should be 2.5’ X 4’ (width X height).
  • Poster material: PVC banner.
  • Purpose and objective of the study/project/work/research must be mentioned.
  • Participants may use their thesis topic as the subject of the poster.
  • Sources and references of the data should be provided in the poster.

Evaluation:

Poster or project will be evaluated based on the innovation, research work and presentation.

N.B :

  1. Any violation of rules or misbehave may lead to disqualification of the participant or team.
  2. In any case, judge’s decision will be declared final.
  3. Organizing committee reserves all the right to change any rules.
